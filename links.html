Edain Community Links

Edain Mod has many places where community interacts, looking for games, discussing the mod, watching pro players or simply having fun. I will try to keep this page updated and representative of the various youtube channels, discord servers, and other interesting places for any player. If you find I missed something, please contact me in Discord to solve it :D

Official Edain channels

Edain has some official places to announce news, provide download links and recieve feedback, namely:
Modding Union: Edain's forum is a big place, appropiate for bug reports, suggestions of all kind, proposals, etc. It is also the place to showcase new features and publish news about the mod.
ModDB: One of the most visited sites, and the main download section, also contains fan material such as custom maps, submod announcements, etc.
Edain's Youtube Channel: Streams, feature videos, trailers... Stay tuned!

Discord Servers

Although unofficial, Edain has 3 main servers where players and fans talk, find games, and know each other.
Edain Fight Club: Edain's pvp focused server. Most, if not all of the competitive players inhabit this server. There are memes, replays, and endless balance talks, maybe memes themselves. Managed by the legendary player Spartacus, also my master and mentor.
Edain Community (EN): Edain's english-speaking server, where all kind of players and fans find their place. Participate in debates about concepts, learn more about the game, or chat with fellow fans.
Edain Group (DE): Edain's german-speaking server, for all fans of the game. More memes, their own ranking system, and a big community ready to welcome you. Non-german speakers are welcome, although most discussions are in german.

Wiki

Edain's Wiki is the best place to learn about the game's units, buildings, and also has guides (map creation, strategies, fighting AI...) and other resources for fans. It is specially useful to consult the stats of the units, such as price, health, armorset, etc. The wiki team works hard to keep everything updated, well organized and well written.

Youtube Channels

  • Edain Fight Club: originally created by Spartacus, now also shared with Luke, it offers streams and videos with commentary from top players. The content is diverse, ranging from casts to live games and anything in between: tutorials, series (Is it Imba or do I suck?), tournament coverage... Some SC2 content can be found here too!
  • Goodfella's channel: One of the best Edain players and youtubers, who also enjoys playing chess, SC2, CoH2... Enjoy the british accent of Edain's Queen, its memes and his intrincate thought processes.
  • DSS's channel: The former Edain Meme God offers casts of games, and awesome videos analyzing Edain's core mechanics, spells, reviews of new updates, all with great detail, experienced players as guests, and detailed explanations about the code and the practical side of it.
  • Naodline's channel: Features content from many LOTR games, mainly Third Age Total War and BFME series and mods. Campaign Playthroughs, Sieges, custom games and more!
  • Piggy's channel: Our favourite american youtuber, plays Edain and other BFME versions in all its formats (1v1, team games, FFAs...), in livestreams and recorded videos.
  • Ruuddevil's channel: One of the most viewed and known Edain youtubers, his content includes everything you can imagine: casted games, pvp 1v1, team games, FFAs, custom maps, campaigns, challenges, mod reviews... all spiced up with Ruuddevil's funny commentary.
  • Calger's channel: A different channel, focused around small VS battles between heros and units. Will Aragorn beat Gorthaur in a straight up fight? How do orc archers trade with Ithilien Rangers? Discover it here!
  • Sturmhart's channel (DE): German youtuber Sturmhart (known for his love of outpost rushing) produces varied content, with focus on pvp and teamgames, FFAs, etc.
  • Caun's channel (DE): Nice channel with casts, many varied games and awesome tournament coverage, also features AOTR content and Total War-LOTR games.
